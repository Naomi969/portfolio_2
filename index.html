About Me

My name is Emmett Rushin. I'm currently attending the UNC Chapel Hill Full-Stack boot camp. Coming from a self-taught background in cyber security, I was curious to learn more. I've always enjoyed combining IT and art; thus, I feel web development is the perfect combination of both. When working with clients, I truly strive to deliver exactly what they want and try to go above their expectations. In a group setting, I enjoy working as a team to achieve both quality and artistry when it comes time to deliver.

Outside of IT, I am a self-taught drummer. I also am a singer/song-writer. Writing music has been a big part of my life whether behind a drum-kit or pen to paper. I also enjoy the challenge of video games and solving different puzzles. I often find myself in a pickle and enjoy figuring my way out of it.
